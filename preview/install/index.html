Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the app link above in Chrome.
  2. Wait for the “Install app” banner or tap the menu ⋮ button.
  3. Select Add to Home screen and confirm.

iOS (Safari)

  1. Open the app link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share icon and choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Confirm the name and tap Add.
